THE BURN ENGINE

Dying isn't the end in the 2bitArcade — reviving mid-run costs 1,000 $2BA. But those tokens aren't transferred to some "burn wallet" where they technically still exist. Every revive executes the SPL Token burn instruction: the tokens are destroyed at the mint itself, and the total supply of $2BA literally decreases on-chain. Open the mint on Solscan and watch the supply number fall with every death defied. The counter at the top of this page reads that same on-chain figure live.

WHY SHRINKING SUPPLY MATTERS

Supply only moves in one direction: down. Every revive, by every player, in every game, permanently removes $2BA from existence. The tokens in your wallet therefore represent a continuously growing share of everything that exists — the more the arcade is played, the scarcer $2BA becomes. Scarcity is one side of the value equation; demand is the other. Nothing here is financial advice — verify the burn yourself on-chain and draw your own conclusions.
